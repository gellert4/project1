CASE FILE #001 - THE MIDNIGHT MURDER

========================================
LOCATION: Baskerville Manor
DATE: January 15, 2024
DETECTIVE: Holmes
========================================

WITNESS STATEMENT: Mrs. Hudson (Housekeeper)
Interview Date: January 15, 2024, 9:00 AM

Q: Where were you on the night of the murder?
A: I was in my quarters reading a book. I retired at 10 PM and didn't leave my room until morning.

Q: Did you hear anything unusual?
A: Yes, I heard a loud noise around 11:30 PM. Sounded like something fell in the main study.

Q: Who do you suspect?
A: The butler has been acting strangely lately. He's been arguing with the deceased about unpaid wages.

========================================

WITNESS STATEMENT: The Butler (Edward Watson)
Interview Date: January 15, 2024, 10:30 AM

Q: Where were you at the time of the murder (11:30 PM)?
A: I was asleep in my quarters. I had worked late that evening and went to bed at 10 PM.

Q: Did you have any conflicts with the deceased?
A: Yes, sir. He owed me three months' wages. I was frustrated, but I would never harm him.

Q: Can anyone verify your whereabouts?
A: No, sir. I was alone in my room.

========================================

WITNESS STATEMENT: The Gardener (James Murphy)
Interview Date: January 15, 2024, 11:00 AM

Q: Where were you at the time of the murder?
A: I was leaving the manor. I had worked late on the grounds. I left through the east gate around 11:15 PM.

Q: Did you see anyone leaving the manor?
A: I saw someone running from the study window around 11:25 PM. It was dark, but the figure moved quickly.

Q: Could you identify the person?
A: No, too dark. But they wore a dark coat and hat.

========================================

PHYSICAL EVIDENCE

- Victim found in study at 6:00 AM
- Weapon: Candlestick (silver, from the study)
- Window locks: Study window was unlocked
- Footprints: Muddy footprints leading from study to east exit
- No signs of forced entry

========================================

SUSPECT PROFILE 1: The Butler (Edward Watson)
- Age: 52
- Employment: 15 years at the manor
- Motive: Unpaid wages (3 months owed)
- Alibi: Unconfirmed (asleep in quarters)
- Evidence: None found in room, but had access to all areas

========================================

SUSPECT PROFILE 2: The Gardener (James Murphy)
- Age: 35
- Employment: 5 years
- Motive: Unknown (no conflicts known)
- Alibi: Says he was leaving at 11:15 PM
- Evidence: Saw someone leaving the study, muddy footprints match his shoes

========================================

INVESTIGATOR NOTES

The case is complex. While the gardener claims to have seen someone fleeing, his muddy footprints are found at the scene. The butler has a clear motive but an unverified alibi. Mrs. Hudson's account of hearing noise at 11:30 PM correlates with both the gardener's timeline and the estimated time of death.

Need to verify: Butler's whereabouts, Gardener's exact departure time, and timeline of events more carefully.
